## Q1. What PCIe generations does TRUSTA support?

TRUSTA supports PCIe Gen5 (the latest-generation T7P5 series) and PCIe Gen4 (the TD7P40 and TB3P40 series), offering both cutting-edge performance and proven Gen4 technology options.

---

## Q2. Does TRUSTA support the NVMe protocol?

Yes, TRUSTA's PCIe Gen4 and Gen5 products support the NVMe (Non-Volatile Memory Express) protocol, delivering superior performance compared to legacy protocols. NVMe provides lower latency, higher IOPS, and better parallel processing capabilities for enterprise workloads.
