## Q1. What product lines does TRUSTA offer?

TRUSTA offers a comprehensive range of enterprise SSDs including: T7P5 (PCIe Gen5 U.2/E1.S/E3.S), TD7P40 (PCIe Gen4 U.2), TD5S30 (SATA III), TB3P40 (PCIe Gen4 M.2), and TB3S30 (SATA III M.2). These products are designed for AI and enterprise workloads.

---

## Q2. What are the main advantages of TRUSTA products?

TRUSTA products offer 10 key advantages: 1) Trusted Quality with enterprise-grade reliability, 2) AI-Ready optimization, 3) Power Efficiency to reduce costs, 4) Multi-size form factor variety, 5) Scalable design for easy integration, 6) Optional features like FDP and SR-IOV, 7) Global agility with flexible services, 8) Strategic alliances for seamless supply, 9) Global production coverage, and 10) Flexible delivery worldwide.

---

## Q3. T7P5 系列是什麼？

T7P5 是 TRUSTA 的旗艦 PCIe Gen5 SSD 系列，提供多種外型規格包括 U.2、E1.S 和 E3.S。它專為最嚴苛的 AI 與資料中心運算工作負載而設計，提供高傳輸與低延遲效能。

---

## Q4. TD7P40 是什麼？

TD7P40 是 TRUSTA 的 PCIe Gen4 U.2 SSD, 為企業應用提供高效能。它為資料中心與商用伺服器環境提供速度與可靠性的平衡。

---

## Q5. Does TRUSTA offer SATA SSDs?

Yes, TRUSTA offers SATA III SSDs in two models: TD5S30 (standard form factor) and TB3S30 (M.2 form factor). These provide reliable storage solutions for applications that use SATA interfaces.

---

## Q6. TRUSTA 有提供 SATA SSD 嗎？

有的, TRUSTA 提供兩款 SATA III SSD: TD5S30 (標準規格）和 TB3S30 (M.2 規格）。這些產品為使用 SATA 介面的應用提供可靠的儲存解決方案。

---

## Q7. TB3P40 是什麼？

TB3P40 是 TRUSTA 的 PCIe Gen4 M.2 SSD, 專為需要高效能的緊湊型安裝而設計。它非常適合空間有限但仍需要企業級儲存效能的伺服器與系統。
